How does it Work?
For each item, you only need 3 steps.

Take a Picture of the item

Or upload a picture of the item. Our AI will recognize the picture, describe it, categorize it, and recommend products.

Price Tag picture

From the price Tag, Brand, Size, Price, Model and SKU number will be recognized if available.

Review Results

Check that the results match your expectations.
